# Idea Brief: MeetingCost Team SaaS

**ID:** WE-20260621-meetingcost-team
**Date:** 2026-06-21
**Status:** Candidate

## Problem
Knowledge workers spend 31 hrs/mo in meetings; 30%+ are unnecessary (Atlassian 2024). For 50 employees at $50/hr, wasted meeting cost exceeds $38K/mo. Free calculators exist (MeetCalc, Koalendar) but lack team analytics. Calwise charges €30–150/mo for calendar-integrated cost overlays. Wealth-engine MeetingCost has viral embed widget + SEO pages (`/p/meeting-cost-calculator.html`, `/go/meeting.html`).

## Solution
Launch **MeetingCost Team** at **$29/mo** (up to 25 seats):
- Shared team salary templates + meeting history dashboard
- Embeddable widget for company intranet (existing embed at `/embed/meeting.html`)
- Export PDF reports ($5 one-time already exists — bundle into sub)
- Viral loop: free calculator → email capture → team trial

## Monetization
$29/mo × 1,724 teams = $50k MRR. Lower-friction path: 500 teams + 5K/mo one-time report sales at $5. Calwise validates €30/mo entry for org analytics.

## Automation Angle
Calculator, embed code generation, Stripe billing automated. Horseshoe cross-traffic for viral distribution. SEO pages already live.

## Competition
AgendaBurn Pro $4.99/mo (consumer), Calwise €30/mo (enterprise calendar sync). Gap: indie team tier with embed viral loop, no calendar OAuth required for MVP.

## Validation Plan
Add team dashboard MVP; post embed widget to r/productivity; target 10 team trials in 30 days.

## Risks and Guardrails
Salary data is sensitive — store locally or encrypted; clear privacy policy. No employee surveillance claims.
