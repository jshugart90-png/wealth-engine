Profit Margin Calculator Pro helps freelancers and small business owners instantly see whether a project, product, or service is actually profitable — with gross margin, net margin, markup, and dollar profit breakdowns.

Enter revenue, cost of goods, and operating expenses to get a clear picture of your margins. Visual progress bars and health indicators show at a glance if your pricing is sustainable.

Features:
• Gross and net profit margin percentages
• Dollar profit breakdown (gross profit, net profit)
• Markup percentage on cost of goods
• Revenue presets for quick what-if scenarios
• Margin health indicator (strong, healthy, thin, loss)
• Last calculation saved locally on your device
• Works offline — no account required

Perfect for solopreneurs, Etsy sellers, agency owners, and anyone pricing products or services without spreadsheet headaches.
