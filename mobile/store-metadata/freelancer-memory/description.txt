Freelancer Memory is a classic card-matching puzzle featuring your favorite freelancer tools!

Flip cards to find matching pairs — BillSnap, NDAGen, StatusPing, HookRelay, and more. Beat your fewest-moves record with simple tap controls.

Features:
• 8 tool pairs on a 4×4 grid
• Fewest moves saved on your device
• Works offline after first load
• Family-friendly, all ages
• No account or signup required
• Links to real freelancer tools

Perfect for quick brain breaks between client calls. Part of the Horseshoe Games collection by Wealth Engine.
