Bill Splitter Pro helps you split restaurant bills, group dinners, and shared expenses with tip and per-person breakdown — instantly.

Enter the bill subtotal, tip percentage, and number of people to see each person's share, total with tip, and tip per person. Pro unlock adds tax/service charge line, round-up totals, and uneven custom splits when people ordered different amounts.

Features:
• Equal bill split with tip presets (0%, 18%, 20%, 25%)
• Per-person total and tip breakdown
• Split between 1–20 people with +/- controls
• Grand total, subtotal, and tip summary
• Share split summary with friends
• Last calculation saved locally on your device
• Works offline — no account required
• Pro: add tax or service charge before tip
• Pro: round total up to nearest dollar
• Pro: uneven split with custom share weights per person

Perfect for restaurants, group outings, roommates, and travel — no mental math or spreadsheet needed.
