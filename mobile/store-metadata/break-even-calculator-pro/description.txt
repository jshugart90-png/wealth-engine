Break-Even Calculator Pro helps freelancers and small business owners find exactly how many sales or units they need to cover fixed costs — with contribution margin, break-even revenue, and profit buffer built in.

Enter fixed costs, price per unit, and variable cost per unit to instantly see your break-even point. Visual bars and health indicators show whether your pricing leaves enough room to cover overhead.

Features:
• Break-even units and revenue at a glance
• Contribution margin per unit and percentage
• +10% profit buffer unit target
• Fixed cost presets for quick what-if scenarios
• Margin health indicator (strong, healthy, thin, invalid)
• Last calculation saved locally on your device
• Works offline — no account required

Perfect for product sellers, service providers, Etsy shops, and anyone launching a side business without spreadsheet headaches.
