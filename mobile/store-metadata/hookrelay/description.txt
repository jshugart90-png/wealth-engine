• Durable receive → retry → DLQ → replay dashboard
• Email + Slack alerts when delivery fails 3×
• Payload inspector for fast debugging
• Free tier: 1 endpoint, 1K events/mo

• Works offline after first load for docs and pricing

Perfect for developers who need reliable webhook delivery without Zapier bills.
