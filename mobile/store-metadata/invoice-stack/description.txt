Invoice Stack is a satisfying arcade stacking game for freelancers, bookkeepers, and anyone who loves quick skill challenges!

Tap left or right to position falling invoice blocks — align them carefully or they'll topple off the stack. Lose lives on bad drops, level up as your tower grows, and chase your best score!

Features:
• Simple tap controls — left/right movement
• Best score saved on your device
• Works offline after first load
• Family-friendly, all ages
• No account or signup required
• Link to BillSnap PDF invoice tools

Perfect for quick breaks between client work. Part of the Horseshoe Games collection by Wealth Engine.
