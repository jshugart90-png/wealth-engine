Invoice Number Rush is a fast-paced reflex game for freelancers, bookkeepers, and anyone who deals with invoice numbers all day!

A target invoice number appears at the top — tap the matching number when it pops up in the grid. Build streaks for bonus points, dodge decoys, and beat the clock as the pace speeds up.

Features:
• Simple tap controls — match before numbers vanish
• Streak bonuses and increasing difficulty
• Best score saved on your device
• Works offline after first load
• Family-friendly, all ages
• No account or signup required
• Links to BillSnap invoice tools

Perfect for quick breaks between client work. Part of the Horseshoe Games collection by Wealth Engine.
