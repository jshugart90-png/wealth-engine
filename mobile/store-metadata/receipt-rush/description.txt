Receipt Rush is a fast-paced arcade game — catch falling receipts before they hit the ground!

Move your basket left and right to collect good receipts and dodge crumpled ones. Miss five and it's game over. Beat your best score and chase a new high!

Features:
• Simple tap-and-drag controls
• Best score saved on your device
• Works offline after first load
• Family-friendly, all ages
• No account or signup required
• Link to free BillSnap invoice tools

Perfect for quick breaks. Part of the Horseshoe Games collection by Wealth Engine.
