Webhook Whack is a fast-paced whack-a-mole arcade game for developers and anyone who loves quick reflex challenges!

Tap the failed webhooks (🐛) before they disappear — but don't whack the healthy ones (✅) or you'll lose points. Beat the clock, chase your best score, and see if you can become a webhook hero!

Features:
• Simple tap controls — 3×3 grid
• Best score saved on your device
• Works offline after first load
• Family-friendly, all ages
• No account or signup required
• Link to HookRelay webhook retry tools

Perfect for quick breaks between deploys. Part of the Horseshoe Games collection by Wealth Engine.

Privacy: Scores stay on your device. See our privacy policy for details.
