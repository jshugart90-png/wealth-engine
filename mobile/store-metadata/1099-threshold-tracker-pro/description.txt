1099 Threshold Tracker Pro helps small businesses and freelancers track contractor payments toward the federal $600 1099-NEC filing threshold — with W-9 status, multi-vendor lists, and filing alerts.

Add vendors, log year-to-date payments, and instantly see who needs a 1099-NEC form. Progress bars show how close each contractor is to the threshold. Flag missing W-9s before year-end.

Features:
• Multi-vendor payment tracking with local save
• Configurable threshold (default $600 federal)
• W-9 on-file status per contractor
• Summary: total vendors, 1099s required, YTD spend
• Works offline — data stays on your device

Perfect for solopreneurs, agencies, and bookkeepers managing 1099 compliance without spreadsheet headaches.
